Meet The Artist

Hello! my name is Erin, also known as Reinboee (pronounced "Rainbow"). I am a self-taught digital artist based in the US and have been drawing since I was young. Growing up, I have always enjoyed creating things, whether it be crafting houses for my dolls, stop-motion filmmaking, or simply doodling people. Contrary to how my name Reinboee is pronounced as "Rainbow", my persona is a black and white cat, and I now mostly draw in a greyscale color palette. This juxtaposition along with the peculiar spelling of "Reinboee" reflects the uniqueness and individuality of art that I love.
